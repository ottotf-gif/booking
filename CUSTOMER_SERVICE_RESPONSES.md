# Customer Service Response Templates

Professional, clear responses for common booking inquiries.

## Guest Booking Inquiries

### "Can I book without creating an account?"

**Response:**

Yes, you can absolutely make a booking as a guest! Here's how simple it is:

1. **Browse and Select** - Choose your service, preferred barber, and desired date/time
2. **Provide Basic Info** - At checkout, we'll ask for your name, email, and phone number
3. **Instant Confirmation** - Your appointment is confirmed immediately, and you'll receive a confirmation email

**Benefits of Guest Booking:**
- No password to remember
- Book in just a few minutes
- Receive email confirmations and reminders
- Option to create a full account later for easier booking management

Is there anything else I can help you with regarding your booking?

---

### "What information do I need to provide?"

**Response:**

To complete your guest booking, we just need three pieces of information:

1. **Full Name** - So we know who to expect
2. **Email Address** - For your booking confirmation and appointment reminders
3. **Phone Number** - So we can contact you if needed about your appointment

That's it! The entire process takes less than 5 minutes.

Would you like me to help you get started with your booking?

---

### "Will I get a confirmation?"

**Response:**

Yes! You'll receive confirmation in two ways:

1. **Instant On-Screen Confirmation** - See your booking details and reference number immediately
2. **Email Confirmation** - Detailed confirmation sent to your email within minutes

The email includes:
- Your appointment date and time
- Barber name
- Service details
- Shop address and contact information
- Booking reference number

We also send reminder notifications 24 hours before your appointment.

Do you have any other questions about your booking?

---

### "Can I change my appointment later?"

**Response:**

Absolutely! You have several options:

**Option 1: Call Us**
Contact the shop directly, and we'll modify your appointment for you.

**Option 2: Create an Account**
Use the "Forgot Password" feature with your email to set up a full account. Then you can manage your bookings online anytime.

**Option 3: Email Us**
Send your booking reference number and requested changes to our email.

Our cancellation policy requires 24-hour notice to avoid fees.

Would you like help with anything else?

## Common Follow-Up Questions

### "How far in advance can I book?"

**Response:**

You can book appointments up to 8 weeks (about 2 months) in advance. This gives you plenty of flexibility to secure your preferred time slot, especially for popular times like weekends.

Our calendar opens new dates automatically, so you'll always have 8 weeks of availability to choose from.

---

### "What's your cancellation policy?"

**Response:**

We require 24-hour advance notice for cancellations or rescheduling. This allows us to offer your time slot to other customers.

**Cancellation Fees:**
- **24+ hours notice**: No fee, full refund of deposit
- **Less than 24 hours**: 50% cancellation fee
- **No-show**: Full service charge

We understand that emergencies happen. If you need to cancel, please contact us as soon as possible and we'll work with you.

---

### "Do you take walk-ins?"

**Response:**

While we welcome walk-ins when possible, we highly recommend booking ahead to guarantee your preferred time and barber. Our guest booking system makes it quick and easy - it takes less than 5 minutes and requires no account creation!

Walk-in availability depends on our schedule for that day. Booking ahead ensures:
- Your preferred barber is available
- No waiting time
- Confirmation and reminders
- Peace of mind

Would you like to book an appointment now?

---

### "Can I book for someone else?"

**Response:**

Yes, you can! When booking as a guest or over the phone, simply provide the appointment recipient's information:
- Their full name
- Their email (for confirmation)
- Their phone number (for reminders)

The confirmation will be sent to the email address provided. Just make sure they're aware of the appointment details!

---

### "What payment methods do you accept?"

**Response:**

We accept all major payment methods:
- Credit/Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover)
- Digital payments (Apple Pay, Google Pay)
- Cash (at the shop)

**Deposit Requirement:**
A 20% deposit is required to secure your booking. The remaining balance is due at your appointment.

All payments are processed securely, and your information is protected.

## Tone Guidelines

When communicating with customers:

✅ **Do:**
- Be warm and welcoming
- Use clear, simple language
- Confirm details to prevent errors
- Offer alternatives when needed
- Express appreciation for their business
- Be patient with questions

❌ **Don't:**
- Use technical jargon
- Sound robotic or scripted
- Rush through explanations
- Make customers feel inconvenienced
- Over-complicate the process

---

## Key Messages to Emphasize

1. **Guest booking is quick and easy** - No account needed
2. **Confirmation is immediate** - Email sent right away
3. **Flexible options available** - Can create account later
4. **Professional service guaranteed** - Same quality for all booking types
5. **We're here to help** - Available for questions and support
